Client Project

The final client project was a 6-weeks project where I worked with a team of four to redesign a small revived furniture business for the client. The client had an existing website that was hard to maintain and would like a new look. Our purpose was to create a user-friendly website using Wordpress and completely redesign the whole website.

Meeting / Planning Phase

We started off doing some research on the business and met with our client to discover her concerns and her idea for the new website. After the meeting, we started to plan out the information architecture, content lists, and wireframe for the website. After finalizing the wireframe with our client, we created a mock-up using Adobe Illustrator on the new look of the website and presented to our client.

Wordpress Development Phase

We created several custom post types, custom taxonomies, and advanced custom fields. During the development phase, our team worked individually on different pieces of the puzzle. Our team met regularly to discuss the status and any problems we encountered. We also worked together on key components to make sure that all team members agreed on the solution.

Site Testing

The site testing was conducted with three users. The purpose of this test was to determine whether our targeted audience can easily navigate the site and whether the user interface made sense to our users. We wanted to identify all underlying problems and possible improvements for the site during the site testing stage so we can make improvements and deliver the end product successfully to our client. One major change we made after the site test was creating a separate taxonomy for the furniture sale status on the "Furniture Page" and edited our isotope.js file to accompany this change.

Final Deliverable

We met with our client to showcase our end result for the newly designed web page. We also educated our client on how to maintain the website using the WordPress dashboard.
